The Juicy Frog Drink

Description

The juicy frog is a delicious, not-too-sweet, not-too-tart drink, ready for any party! Approved by rowdy French-Canadian women! Add more 7up® to taste!

Ingredients

  • 3 cubes ice
  • ½ (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
  • ½ (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger triple sec
  • ½ (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger lime cordial
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger sweet and sour mix
  • ½ cup lemon-lime soda (such as 7-Up®)

Steps

  1. Place ice cubes in a glass and top with vodka, triple sec, lime cordial, sweet and sour mix, and lemon-lime soda, respectively. Mix well.
